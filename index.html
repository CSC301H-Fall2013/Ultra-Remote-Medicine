Welcome

Ultra-Remote-Medicine is an initiative to help mothers in developing countries deliver babies safely in an effort to reduce both infant and maternal mortality. The team's pilot project is to use portable ultrasound machines to evaluate pregnant women in order to diagnose potential problems before they can become life-threatening.
